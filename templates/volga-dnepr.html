I started my career on a project at the university, which was a collaboration with Volga-Dnepr air-cargo company. A group of teachers, graduate students, and students worked on a system to predict and prevent aviation incidents. We analyzed various factors, such as crew fatigue, weather conditions, and aircraft technical condition, providing assessments on managerial decisions to reduce potential expenses per flight from $2000 to $500. We utilized specially designed mathematical models for incident prediction.

Initially, I spent a short time programming in C# in the team, but it turned out there was no system administrator, so I took on that role. I first introduced Git into the development process. I migrated PHP code to Ubuntu 11.10, and the project lasted until around 2019 when it concluded. Additionally, I performed the installation of the entire system, including the OS and PHP, without internet access. I had to download necessary deb packages and write an installation bash script.
